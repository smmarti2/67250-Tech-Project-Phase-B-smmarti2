Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the world of books and storytelling, and how they shape human creativity and culture. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive displays, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover the power of literature—from classic works to modern voices—and how stories influence the way we think, imagine, and connect. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning guest, our exhibitions offer fresh perspectives on the impact of books in everyday life. Plan your visit to experience engaging literary exhibits, hands-on activities, and events designed to inspire a love for reading and storytelling.
