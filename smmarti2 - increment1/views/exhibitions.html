Exhibitions 1: Brimstone: Callie Hart

Brimstone is a multimedia exhibition by artist Callie Hart that explores themes of transformation, identity, and the human experience through a blend of sculpture, video, and interactive installations. The exhibition invites visitors to immerse themselves in a world where the boundaries between reality and imagination blur, encouraging reflection on the complexities of self-discovery and personal growth.

Exhibitions 2: Exhibitions 2: Alechmized: SenLin Yu

Once a promising alchemist, Helena Marino is now a prisoner — of war and of her own fractured mind. Her captor, Commander Kaine Ferron, is determined to unearth the secrets buried deep within her transmuted memory. Gripping, gothic, and emotionally devastating.
